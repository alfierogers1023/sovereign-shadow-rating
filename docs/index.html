Sovereign Shadow-Rating Dashboard

42 sovereigns, scored from public macro / fiscal / external / governance data and compared against actual S&P / Fitch / Moody's ratings. The analytical payload is the divergence — where the model(s) and the agencies disagree, and why — not a single accuracy number.

Every number below is leave-one-country-out (LOOCV), never in-sample fit. Pillar scaling is percentile rank within this 42-country sample — it will not transfer to a country outside the sample without recalibration. Don't expect either model to beat the agencies on accuracy; ratings are slow and through-the-cycle by design. See CLAUDE.md in the repo for full methodology and caveats.
